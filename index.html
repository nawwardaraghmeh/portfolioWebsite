ABOUT ME

My name is Nawwar Daraghmeh. I'm a Computer Science student at AAUP. I was born and raised in Tubas, Palestine.
Early in life, a rich canvas of hobbies and passions painted my upbringing. The pages of books, the strokes of a pencil, the threads of embroidery, and the rhythm of music all colored my world with creativity. Yet, despite these endeavors, it was the alluring world of computers and software that truly sparked my soul.
From an early age, the allure of technology beckoned. My curiosity was ignited by the coding creativity that transformed simple words into complex software wonders. Each project deepened my passion, pulling me into the ever-evolving landscape of computer science.
This journey isn't just about skill acquisition; it's a symphony of lifelong fascinations blending with an unwavering dedication to the limitless horizons of the digital age.
